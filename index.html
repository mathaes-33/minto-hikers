An Authentic Minto Hiking Community

Connecting people with nature and each other, one local trail at a time.

Minto Hikers is a friendly, community-oriented group dedicated to exploring the robust and actively managed outdoor recreation landscape of Minto, Ontario. Our town is a hub for beautiful local trails like the Clifford Trail, White's Junction, and the Harriston Greenway, and serves as a gateway to the broader Wellington County trail network.
